Terrforma Documentation

Customisable flat world generation for all.

Bored of the same old Minecraft flat world? Superflat not doing it for you? Look no further! Nucleus Terrafirma is one thing - a "create your own flat world generator" plugin.

Want a conventional grass, dirt, stone, bedrock setup? Cool, we can do that? But how about sticking hot magma under the stone? Perhaps a layer of diamonds if they dig deep enough? Maybe you want to prank the foolhardy and NOT add bedrock? Many possibilities, and now, one simple plugin that enables your flat worlds for **any** world management plugin!

Created for you. Open for all.

We're open ears here! Found a bug? Something not quite right? Or do you have an idea that would fit in with Nucleus? Let us know on our GitHub issues page!

Are you a developer who wants to help out! Send us a pull request!

Nucleus Terrafirma is open source and licenced under the MIT licence. That means you can grab the code, tinker with it, and hopefully improve it!
